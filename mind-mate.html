MindMate: Designing a Mental Health Companion App

MindMate is a digital wellness companion designed to help users track their mental health, build emotional resilience, and develop better habits. The app uses AI-powered journaling, guided mood-tracking exercises, and personalized recommendations to provide insights into mental well-being.

Role

Role

UX Designer & Web Designer

Duration

Duration

12 Weeks

Tools

Tools

Figma, Photoshop, Webflow

Team

Team

2 Developers,1 Product Manager

Challenge

Students and young professionals struggle with mental health management, with 67% reporting difficulty balancing academic/work responsibilities with mental wellness. Existing mental health apps either feel too clinical or lack practical daily management tools.

Results

MindMate’s solution involved implementing an AI-powered chatbot that offers mood tracking and guided journaling suggestions. The UI was redesigned to facilitate seamless navigation, making it easier for users to engage with self-care tools. Personalized insights were improved through adaptive learning, ensuring better accuracy in mental health recommendations.

Usability testing for MindMate involved tracking how users interacted with the journaling and AI-generated insights. Testers were asked to complete a guided journaling session and assess whether the AI-generated suggestions aligned with their needs. Feedback highlighted that users found the AI supportive but sometimes impersonal, leading to further refinements in personalized

Interview Questions:

1. How do you currently track your moods?

2. Do you find AI-generated suggestions helpful?

3. What aspects of journaling do you struggle with?

4. How effective do you find guided journaling exercises?

5. What features would improve your daily self-care routine?

6. Do you feel the AI understands your emotional needs?

7. Would you use a chatbot for mood tracking daily?

Outcomes

✅ Beta testing showed 85% daily active user retention
✅ 92% of users had decreased anxiety about tasks.
✅ 78% successfully formed new habits within 21 days

✅ 88% completion rate for mental wellness courses
