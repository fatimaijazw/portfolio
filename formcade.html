Formcade: Building a No-Code Form Solution

Formcade is a modern, no-code form builder designed for effortless data collection and automation. It simplifies the process of creating, managing, and embedding forms, making it an ideal solution for businesses, designers, and developers who want a seamless way to gather insights without complex setups.

Role

Role

UX Designer & Web Designer

Duration

Duration

8 Weeks

Tools

Tools

Figma, Photoshop, Webflow

Team

Team

2 Developers,1 Product Manager

Challenge

Research showed that businesses lose an average of 10-15 hours per week managing form creation and data collection processes. Non-technical teams struggle with form creation, often resulting in delayed projects or expensive developer dependencies. Business users needed a way to create dynamic, customizable forms without coding knowledge, while ensuring these forms integrated seamlessly with their existing workflows and tools.

Results

The redesign of Formcade resulted in a 30% increase in user engagement and a 25% boost in premium sign-ups. User testing showed a 40% reduction in time spent creating forms, while post-launch surveys revealed a 15% increase in user satisfaction, largely due to the more intuitive interface and streamlined navigation. These improvements highlight how the design changes enhanced both user experience and business performance.

Process

Test

Usability Testing

Participants were asked to perform key tasks within the Formcade platform, including creating forms, adding different field types, and navigating through the platform’s features. We observed how users approached each task, the time taken to complete each action, and any feedback they provided during the session.

Interview Questions:

  1. How easy was it to create and customize your form in Formcade?

  2. Did you find the form field options (text, dropdown, checkboxes) intuitive and sufficient?

  3. Was the process of submitting and viewing form responses clear and straightforward?

  4. How simple was it to share your form with others

  5. Were any parts of Formcade confusing or hard to navigate?

  6. What additional features or improvements would enhance your experience with Formcade?

  7. How likely would you be to recommend Formcade to others, and why?
