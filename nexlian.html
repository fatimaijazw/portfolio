Nexlian: Empowering Healthcare with Next-Gen AI Solutions

Nexlian is an AI-enhanced collaborative workspace designed to streamline team workflows and boost productivity. It integrates automation, real-time collaboration, and intelligent task management to optimize work efficiency.


Nexlian is an AI-enhanced collaborative workspace designed to streamline team workflows and boost productivity. It integrates automation, real-time collaboration, and intelligent task management to optimize work efficiency.

Role

Role

Tools

Challenge

Healthcare providers lose 30% of their time to administrative tasks and data management, while struggling to maintain HIPAA compliance. Existing healthcare software often feels outdated and fails to leverage modern AI capabilities effectively. Healthcare providers needed a modern, AI-powered platform to streamline operations, ensure compliance, and improve patient engagement while maintaining data security.

Results

Nexlian introduced a smart automation assistant that aids in workflow optimization, reducing repetitive tasks. The UI was refined for consistency and improved user adoption. Integration with external productivity tools was expanded, allowing users to consolidate their work environment within the platform.

Usability testing for Nexlian focused on task automation and real-time collaboration. Users were asked to complete a workflow using automation and provide feedback on efficiency. Results showed that while automation features improved task completion rates, some users faced challenges integrating third-party tools effectively.
